01

Your Brain Is One Of The Most Energy-Hungry Organs In Your Body

Every decision. Every presentation. Every conversation. Every problem you solve.

They all require enormous amounts of energy. That energy is produced inside your cells, and NAD+ plays an essential role in that process. From around your mid-30s onwards, NAD levels naturally decline. By age 50, they're significantly lower than they were in early adulthood.

For people whose careers depend on quick thinking, this gradual change can become surprisingly noticeable. You may recognise:

  • Needing longer to process complex information
  • Slower recall during conversations

  • Feeling mentally "flat" even after a weekend

This isn't about intelligence. It's about supporting the systems your brain relies on every day.
