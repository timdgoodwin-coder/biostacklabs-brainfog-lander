Brain Fog Is Often Mitochondrial Underperformance

Neurons are extremely energy-demanding cells. They rely heavily on mitochondria — your cellular "power plants." NAD is a core driver of mitochondrial function and electron transport.

When NAD is depleted:

Mitochondria produce less ATP
Neural signalling becomes inefficient
Cognitive speed drops
In simple terms: your brain is still "online," but it's buffering. Think of using an 8-year-old laptop versus a new one — everything happens slower, and it's frustrating.

Support neuronal resilience
